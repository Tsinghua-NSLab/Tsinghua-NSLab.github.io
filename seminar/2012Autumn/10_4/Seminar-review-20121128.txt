In Tor, if your entry node and exit node are compromised, you are sunk. If this is the case, what is the point of circuits with more then 2 hops?
One of the tensions for Tor (and other anonymity systems) is that they need a good user base to improve the system. However, the anonymity the offer isn’t great. How do you get people to use such a system?
