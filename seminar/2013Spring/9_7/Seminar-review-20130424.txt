Presentation[edit]Problem: How to conceal Tor traffic from censorship?
Censors could detect Tor traffic by checking packet size
Solution: Disguise Tor traffic as an innocuous cover protocol, such as P2P and HTTP
Say steganography:
Hide a hiddentext in a covertext
An adversary shouldn’t be able to tell that the hiddentext is present
Assumption
Censor doesn’t install spyware on users’ computers
Censor doesn’t operate Tor relays
Censor wishes to avoid collateral damage
Censor has to make decisions at backbone scale
Implementation
Chopping + Steganography at both StegoTorus Client and Server side
Evaluation
StegoTorus + chopping: close to the performance of vanilla Tor
StegoTorus + chopping + HTTP Steganography: goodput is about 30KB/s; both connection length and connection payload are similar to vanilla TorQuestion:
