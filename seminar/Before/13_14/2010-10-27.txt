Content:
Background
Data Center Transport RequirementsReview
TCP/ECN Control LoopData Center TCP Algorithm
1. Two Key Ideas
1)	React in proportion to the extent of congestion, not its presence.
2)	Mark based on instantaneous queue length.
2. Algorithm
1)	Switch side: Mark packets when Queue Length > K
2)	Sender side: Maintain running average of fraction of packets marked (alpha)
3. Why it works
1)	High Burst Tolerance
2)	Low Latency
3)	High Throughput
4. Analysis
How low can DCTCP maintain queues without loss of throughput?
How do we set the DCTCP parameters?
5. Evaluation
1)	Implemented in Windows stack
2)	Real hardware, 1Gbps and 10Gbps experiments
3)	Numerous benchmarks
6. Implementation
1)	Low latency for short flows
2)	High throughput for long flows
3)	High burst tolerance for query flowsConclusion
1. DCTCP satisfies all our requirements for Data Center packet transport.
