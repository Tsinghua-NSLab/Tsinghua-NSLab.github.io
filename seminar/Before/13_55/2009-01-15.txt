About the paper:
	Topic：A Policy-aware Switching Layer for Data Centers
	From：SIGCOMM 2008
	Author：Dilip Joseph
	Why to choose?
	Data center is and will be very important!
The content of the paper
Problem
	Middleboxes are hard to deploy -- On path placement fails to achieve.
	Give some examples to show:
	Flexibility 
		(Re)configurable network topology
	Efficiency
		No middlebox resource wastage
	Correctness 
		Guaranteed middlebox traversal
Solution
	Overview
		Two principles:
		1.	Take middleboxes off-path
		2.	Separate policy from reachability 
		Four phases:
		1.	PSwitch explicitly forwards packets to middleboxes
		2.	Distributed forwarding 
		3.	Loadbalancing middleboxes 
               4.	Different policies for different traffic 
Challenges
	1.	Minimizing infrastructure changes
	2.	Non-transparent middleboxes 
	3.	Guaranteeing correctness under churn
Limitations
	1.	Indirect paths
	2.	Policy specification complexity
Implementation & evaluation
	Compared to software Ethernet switch
		82% TCP throughput
		16% latency increase
Related work
Conclusion
	Deploying middleboxes  is hard
	A new layer-2 with explicit middlebox support
